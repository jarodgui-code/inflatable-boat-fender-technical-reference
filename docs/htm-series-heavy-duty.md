## Structural Design Overview

HTM Series inflatable PVC boat fenders are engineered for heavy-duty marina and commercial docking environments.

Compared to standard cylindrical or pear-shaped models, HTM fenders feature thicker wall construction and reinforced eye structures designed to withstand higher lateral pressure and prolonged compression.

The structural emphasis is on durability and long-term deformation resistance.

---

## Core Technical Advantages

- Heavy wall thickness
- Reinforced hanging eye design
- Increased compression tolerance
- Long-term shape recovery performance
- Suitable for higher displacement vessels

HTM Series is optimized for vessels that remain docked for extended periods or operate in high-traffic marina environments.

---

## Ideal Applications

HTM Series is commonly selected for:

- Large yachts
- Marina berthing zones
- Charter fleets
- High-load docking scenarios
- Commercial small vessel fleets

The design performs reliably under repeated side pressure and variable docking angles.

## HTM Series vs Standard Cylindrical Fenders

| Feature | HTM Series | Standard Cylindrical |
|----------|------------|---------------------|
| Wall Thickness | Heavy-duty | Standard |
| Compression Resistance | High | Moderate |
| Reinforced Eye | Enhanced | Standard |

HTM models are typically chosen when structural endurance and durability are primary selection factors.
