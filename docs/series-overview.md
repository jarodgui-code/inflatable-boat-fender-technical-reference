# Inflatable PVC Boat Fender Series Overview (A / F / D / G / HTM)

**Full guide:** https://seamanmarinesupplies.com/inflatable-pvc-boat-fender-types-explained-a-f-s-d-g-and-htm-series/  
**Last Updated:** 2026-02  

This document summarizes the structural differences and application logic behind the most common inflatable PVC boat fender series used in docking protection.

## Why Series Selection Matters

Fender geometry directly affects:

- Energy absorption behavior
- Contact stability during docking
- Long-term deformation resistance
- Abrasion performance
- Installation flexibility

Choosing the correct structure reduces lifecycle replacement cost and improves vessel protection.

---

## Quick Comparison Matrix

| Series | Shape | Best For | Key Strength | Typical Vessel Range |
|--------|-------|----------|--------------|----------------------|
| A Series | Pear-shaped | General docking | Balanced cushioning | Small–medium vessels |
| F Series | Twin-eye cylindrical | Controlled alignment | Two-point stability | Small craft |
| D Series | Smooth cylindrical | Side mounting | Compact footprint | Small–medium vessels |
| G Series | Ribbed cylindrical | Small boats & abrasion | Progressive load absorption | Small craft |
| HTM Series | Hole-through-middle | High-use marinas | Shape retention & durability | 20–50 ft vessels |
