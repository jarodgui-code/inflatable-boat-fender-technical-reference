## Structural Design Overview

F Series inflatable boat fenders feature a twin-eye cylindrical structure.  
Both ends are reinforced, allowing either vertical or horizontal installation.

Unlike pear-shaped models, the F Series distributes docking impact across a wider horizontal surface, making it particularly suitable for side-to-side berthing and rafting scenarios.

---

## Core Technical Advantages

- Dual reinforced eyes for flexible mounting
- Stable horizontal protection coverage
- Strong compression recovery
- Suitable for larger hull contact areas

The cylindrical body ensures uniform pressure distribution when vessels dock parallel to docks or other boats.

---

## Ideal Docking Applications

F Series fenders are commonly selected for:

- Larger recreational yachts
- Pontoon boats
- Marina berthing lines
- Charter fleets
- Commercial small craft docking

They are especially effective where boats remain tied alongside for extended periods.

---

## A Series vs F Series: Key Structural Difference

| Feature | A Series | F Series |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Shape | Pear-shaped | Cylindrical |
| Mounting | Single eye | Dual eye |
| Best For | General docking | Parallel berthing |
| Impact Spread | Vertical focus | Horizontal distribution |

When side pressure dominates, F Series typically provides more stable hull separation.
