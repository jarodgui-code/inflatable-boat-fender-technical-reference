## Structural Design Overview

D Series inflatable boat fenders feature a straight cylindrical body optimized for consistent surface separation between hull and dock structures.

The balanced cylindrical form provides even compression response and reliable impact buffering during routine docking operations.

Unlike ribbed or pear-shaped models, D Series emphasizes stable linear protection along hull contact points.

---

## Core Technical Advantages

- Uniform cylindrical body
- Predictable compression performance
- Suitable for side-docking and marina berthing
- Balanced energy absorption

The straight-line design helps maintain consistent spacing during extended docking periods.

---

## Ideal Applications

D Series fenders are commonly selected for:

- Small and medium yachts
- Pontoon boats
- Marina berths
- Rental fleets
- Recreational fishing vessels

They are widely used in marina environments where boats remain docked for long durations.

---

## D Series vs F Series: Structural Focus

| Feature | D Series | F Series |
|----------|----------|----------|
| Shape | Straight Cylindrical | Twin-Eye Cylindrical |
| Mounting Flexibility | Standard | Dual orientation |
| Best For | General docking | Heavy side pressure |
| Structural Reinforcement | Moderate | Enhanced eye support |

D Series provides reliable everyday protection, while F Series may be preferred when stronger lateral stability is required.
